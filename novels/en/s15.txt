“What’s happening!?”

The boy had likely, by unfortunate chance, lured a stronger Shamelot toward himself. His small frame swung his knife desperately at the crab, but the cruelly hard shell repelled his blows, and he collapsed onto the rocky ground. The crab’s pincers struck relentlessly.

Grunting from the impact, the boy struggled to rise and swung his knife again. Then—

“…What is this?”

Suddenly, his body began to glow red, flashing with alarm. Startled, he recoiled as the pulsating light enveloped him. Before he could even understand its meaning, he shouted loudly:

“Help me!”

Elz and the others sprinted toward him.

The Shamelot’s pincers struck again just as the boy’s body flashed a warning signal. Even without explanation, the trio could see that danger was imminent.

“Damn it!!”

Seeing the boy thrown to the ground once more, Elz kicked the Shamelot with all his strength. The crab staggered slightly but did not fall—likely a Lv3. This wasn’t an opponent they could take down with brute force alone.

Lins rushed to the boy’s side while Elz refocused on the approaching enemy. Swift joined in, trying to create an opening by attacking its legs.

“Damn… there’s no opening,” Swift muttered.

At that moment, the Shamelot’s vicious pincer caught Elz squarely. He stumbled to his knees, struggling to regain his footing. As he tried to rise, he noticed the familiar red flashing on his own body.

“No… it can’t be…”

His body blinked red—a life-threatening warning. The consecutive battles against Lv3 Shamelots had already pushed them to their limits.

“Elz, get back! I’ll handle this!”

“Don’t be foolish! We can’t beat it alone!”

Despite the warnings, there was no opening to exploit. Their HP drained relentlessly, and soon Swift’s body also began flashing red.

—What do we do? How can we survive this?—

Elz’s mind raced for every possible solution, but with no opening, there were no options left.

“Damn… here of all places!”

Just then, the Shamelot staggered mid-swing at Elz, leaving a brief, precious opening. Swift noticed it immediately.

“Now, take this!!”

Sliding his hands under the Shamelot’s body, Swift flipped it over with sheer force. Elz attacked with all his might while the struggling Shamelot thrashed beneath them. A frantic Lins joined in, shouting:

“Sorry I’m late!”

With all three attacking in unison, the Shamelot finally began to dissolve into particles, its body dispersing into the air.

“We did it…”

The words barely escaped, more like a relieved sigh. Exhausted, the trio watched the radiant light rise from the defeated enemy.

Exchanging glances at their flashing red warnings, Elz and Swift shared a weary, ironic laugh.
